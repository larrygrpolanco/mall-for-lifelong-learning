The Big Takeaways

psychology

Various Learning Strategies

Older adults are strategic learners, not passive users. They employ diverse self-monitoring practices: writing things down by hand, constant self-evaluating, and seeking external resources like ChatGPT or news to supplement app gaps.

auto_awesome

Sustained High Motivation

Unlike younger demographics who often drop off, the 60+ cohort maintained rock-solid motivation for 10 weeks. Their drive is deeply intrinsic: leisure, travel, and a critical focus on maintaining cognitive health and brain plasticity.

replay

The Power of Repetition

While younger learners often criticize repetitive content, older adults actively sought out repetition as a core feature. They redo lessons to achieve mastery, viewing redundancy as an essential tool for memory reinforcement and brain health.
